About me

Hi! My name is Yulia,
I am a professional artist-designer and painter from Ukraine.

I have more than 12 years of experience and I transform spaces with my artistic skills. I know how to select and mix paints, prepare surfaces, apply paints using various techniques.

I graduated from the Faculty of Architectural Design in 2011 and since then I have devoted myself completely to the aesthetics of the environment.

In my work, I always pay attention to details, color balance and proportions. My experience as a decorator and artist enhances the aesthetics and atmosphere of the environment in which you live.

Murals

I painted thousands of murals in Ukraine for restaurants, hotels and private houses. In 2022 I worked for six months in Poland at the company Wakeuptime, where I was a leading artist in painting outdoor advertising on the facades of high-rise buildings
