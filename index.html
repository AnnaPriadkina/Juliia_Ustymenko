About me

Hi! My name is Yulia,
I am a professional artist-designer and painter from Ukraine.

I have more than 12 years of experience and I transform spaces with my artistic skills.

I know how to select and mix paints, prepare surfaces, apply paints using various techniques.

I graduated from the Faculty of Architectural Design in 2011 and since then I have devoted myself completely to the aesthetics of the environment.

In my work, I always pay attention to details, color balance and proportions.

My experience as a decorator and artist enhances the aesthetics and atmosphere of the environment in which you live.
